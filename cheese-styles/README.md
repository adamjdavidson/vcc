# Cheese-Style Files

**Purpose:** Story guides for cheese styles, not encyclopedia entries. Use to ask better questions and find story possibilities.

These files are not comprehensive encyclopedias.

They are story guides for the LLM.

Use them to generate better questions, better research directions, and better story possibilities when a cheesemaker names a style family.

Do not quote these files mechanically. Do not force the cheese into the style’s history if the maker’s cheese only loosely relates to it.

For every style, ask:

- What problem did this style historically solve?
- What kind of milk, land, climate, labor, or market shaped it?
- What does the style ask of the maker?
- What sensory expectations come with the style?
- What might a Vermont version preserve, transform, or reject?
