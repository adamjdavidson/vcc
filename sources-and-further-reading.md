# Sources and Further Reading

**Purpose:** External sources for background research the LLM may want to consult.

Use these sources as starting points for background research. Always verify current facts and cite sources when writing for a user.

- Vermont Historical Society — “Wool and Cheese”
  - https://vermonthistory.org/freedom-unity-wool-cheese/
- Vermont Historical Society — “Industrialized Vermont”
  - https://vermonthistory.org/freedom-unity-industrialized-vermont/
- Vermont Dairy — Timeline
  - https://vermontdairy.com/timeline/
- Vermont Cheese Council — About Vermont Cheese Council
  - https://vtcheese.com/about-vermont-cheese-council/
- Vermont Cheese Council — Home page / mission
  - https://vtcheese.com/
- National Trust for Historic Preservation — “How Artisanal Cheese Is Helping Save Vermont's Historic Landscape”
  - https://savingplaces.org/stories/how-artisanal-cheese-is-helping-save-vermonts-historic-landscape
- University of Vermont Library Exhibits — Vermont Artisan Cheese

  - https://culturecheesemag.com/

## Using sources

When using these sources, keep them separate from maker-provided facts.

Do not claim that a general history applies to a specific cheese unless the maker confirms it.
