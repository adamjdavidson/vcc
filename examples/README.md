# Examples

**Purpose:** Examples teach taste. Don't copy them; learn what works and what fails.

Examples teach the LLM taste.

Do not copy the examples directly. Use them to learn what works and what fails.

Each good story should show:

- what facts it combines,
- what the unifying narrative is,
- what it leaves out,
- who the audience is,
- why someone might become more eager to buy, sell, serve, eat, or share the cheese.

Each bad example should show what generic AI slop sounds like and why it fails.
