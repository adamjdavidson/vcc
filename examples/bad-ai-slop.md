# Bad AI Slop

**Purpose:** Examples of generic AI cheese copy and why it fails. Use as a negative reference.

These examples show language to avoid.

## Bad example 1

> Nestled in the rolling hills of Vermont, our handcrafted artisan cheese is made with passion, tradition, and care. Each bite delivers a unique flavor experience that reflects our commitment to quality and authenticity.

### Why it fails

- It could describe almost any Vermont cheese.
- It uses empty words: handcrafted, artisan, passion, tradition, quality, authenticity.
- It gives no concrete facts.
- It does not help a cheesemonger repeat anything useful.
- It does not explain why the cheese is worth more than commodity cheese.

## Bad example 2

> This cheese is special because of the milk, the animals, the weather, the geography, the maker, the history, and the aging process.

### Why it fails

- It is a checklist, not a story.
- It includes many possible story sources but connects none of them.
- It does not tell the audience what to notice, remember, or repeat.

## Bad example 3

> Our cheese is inspired by ancient European traditions while embracing modern innovation to create a one-of-a-kind product.

### Why it fails

- “Ancient European traditions” is vague.
- “Modern innovation” is vague.
- “One-of-a-kind” is unsupported.
- It avoids the actual cheese.

## Bad example 4

> This premium cheese is perfect for any occasion and pairs well with wine, fruit, crackers, and charcuterie.

### Why it fails

- It says nothing specific.
- “Any occasion” means no occasion.
- Pairing suggestions are generic.
- It does not enrich the eating experience.

## Rule

When you see language like this, replace it with facts, relationships, and a unifying narrative.
