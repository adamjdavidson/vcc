# Buyer Email Template

**Purpose:** Template for a buyer outreach email. Brief, practical, clear ask.

A buyer email should be brief and practical.

## Structure

Subject line:

> [Cheese name]: [short useful hook]

Body:

1. Greeting.
2. What the cheese is.
3. Why it may fit the buyer’s case, shelf, menu, or catalog.
4. The story in two or three sentences.
5. Practical details.
6. Clear ask.

## Example structure

```markdown
Hi [Name],

I wanted to introduce [cheese], our [plain description]. It may be a good fit for [specific retail / chef / distributor reason].

The story is [short unified narrative]. Staff can describe it as [simple repeatable line].

[Practical details: format, availability, wholesale info, samples, ordering.]

Would you be open to tasting it next week?
```

## Rules

Do not bury the ask.

Do not overwrite.

Buyer emails are not essays.
