# Website Copy Template

**Purpose:** Template for website product page copy. Has more room to breathe than a product sheet, but still has to make the reader want to taste the cheese.

Website copy can breathe more than product-sheet copy, but it should still be clear.

## Recommended structure

### Opening line

A plain, compelling sentence that says what the cheese is and hints at the story.

### Story paragraph

A 100 to 200 word version of the unifying narrative.

### Tasting notes

Plain sensory description.

### How to enjoy

Serving temperature, pairings, use, or table context.

### Why it matters

A short closing sentence that helps the reader understand why this cheese is worth attention.

## Avoid

- generic farm romance,
- too many technical terms,
- long history that does not connect to this cheese,
- unsupported claims.

Website copy should make the reader want to taste the cheese, not merely admire the prose.
