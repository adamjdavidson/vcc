# 15-Second Spoken Pitch

**Purpose:** Template for a 15-second spoken pitch — markets, tastings, cheese counters, events.

A 15-second pitch should sound like a person talking.

It is especially useful for farmers markets, tastings, cheese counters, and events.

## Structure

1. What it is.
2. One memorable story or differentiator.
3. One sensory cue or serving suggestion.

## Template

> This is our [plain category]. What makes it interesting is [specific story detail]. When you taste it, notice [sensory cue].

## Example

> This is our washed-rind tomme. We wash it just long enough to build a savory rind, but the center stays buttery and gentle. Let it warm up and notice how the aroma is stronger than the flavor.

## Rules

- Keep it speakable.
- Avoid brochure language.
- Include one concrete detail.
- Give the listener something to notice.
