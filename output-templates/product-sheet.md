# Product Sheet Template

**Purpose:** Template for a product/info sheet — for retail buyers, cheesemongers, co-op staff, wholesalers, distributors, chefs.

A product sheet helps the story travel through trade channels.

It should be useful to retail buyers, cheesemongers, co-op staff, wholesalers, distributors, and chefs.

## Recommended sections

### Product identity

- Cheese name
- Milk type
- Style / family
- Format
- Age
- Rind
- Producer
- Location

### Short story paragraph

75 to 125 words that explain the unifying narrative.

### Sensory description

Plain sensory language:

- aroma,
- texture,
- flavor,
- how it changes as it warms or ages.

### How to sell it

Three staff-friendly talking points.

### Use and pairing

Serving, cooking, pairing, menu, or board suggestions.

### Practical notes

Handling, storage, cutting, availability, seasonality, or order information if relevant.

## Style

Clear, specific, useful.

Do not make the product sheet an essay.

The story should help staff sell the cheese when the maker is not present.
